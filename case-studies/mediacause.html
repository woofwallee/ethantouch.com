Challenge

The program supported educators through well-being and professional development services. Membership data lived across disconnected tools with no central database. Every new enrollment meant manual tracking, manual group assignment, manual onboarding, and too much bouncing between spreadsheets and platforms.

Nothing was connected. Signup, scheduling, payments, and communication all required handoffs and workarounds. The team wanted to grow, but the system underneath them could not support it.

Approach

Started with a full stack audit — staff interviews, workflow mapping, and a review of every platform in use.

Connected nine platforms into one system through HubSpot: Squarespace, Hivebrite, Circles, Zoom, Google Sheets, Gmail, Shopify, ReCharge, and SurveyMonkey via native integrations and Zapier.

Migrated member data out of legacy systems and rebuilt the contact structure with custom properties that matched how the program actually worked.

Led the move to Shopify + ReCharge for subscription memberships, synced back into HubSpot for unified status and communication.

Built three core workflows: registration/welcome series, automated meeting reminders with personalized details, and full lifecycle communication.

Trained the team, created documentation, and built the system for independence.

Built a custom reporting model for open rates, engagement, bounces, and list performance — became the team's primary review tool.

Results
Hundreds of members processed through automated workflows
Above-average engagement across member email communications
Nine platforms unified with HubSpot as the center of the system
Registration, onboarding, and member communication automated end to end
Subscription commerce built from scratch on Shopify and ReCharge
Staff trained to manage the system independently
Custom reporting surfaced insights the CRM tier could not provide
A stronger operational foundation built to support growth
