Recommendations Prioritized actions based on benchmark gaps
High Priority
Clean Customer Segment List
Run a re-engagement campaign for inactive contacts. The 3.30% bounce rate is 3.3x the acceptable ceiling and risks deliverability reputation across all segments.
High Priority
Shorten Email Body Copy
The read-to-glance shift is dramatic across all segments. Move to scannable formats: bullets, bold stats, single clear CTA above the fold.
High Priority
Improve SQL Subject Lines
16.75% open rate means 83% never see content. Test personalization, urgency framing, and question-based subjects. Specificity outperforms generic framing.
Medium Priority
Optimize MQL CTA Design
2.10% click rate with 26.42% opens means content gets seen but doesn't drive action. Test button-style CTAs and reduce links per email.
Medium Priority
Audit MQL Send Cadence
The 0.65% unsubscribe rate (highest segment, 1.6-3.4x benchmark) suggests cadence fatigue. Consider lighter-touch frequency for low-engagement contacts.
Medium Priority
Replicate High-Performing Patterns
Gamified subjects and resource-based content drove 14.97% CTOR (1.5x benchmark). Apply these patterns across underperforming segments.
