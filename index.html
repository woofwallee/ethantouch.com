About

Ethan Touch — HubSpot Specialist

You're looking for someone who can own HubSpot as more than a tool. You need someone who can manage it as an operating system for the teams using it every day: clean architecture, reliable data, tight permissions, documented workflows, cross-team support, and integrations people can actually trust.

I'm Ethan. I'm 4x HubSpot certified across marketing, sales, inbound, and reporting, with seven years in marketing operations and five years owning HubSpot end to end, both in-house and on the agency side.

The problem I usually solve is what happens when HubSpot grows faster than the structure behind it. Properties multiply without governance. Workflows overlap. Sync issues go unresolved. Teams create their own workarounds, and the platform slowly stops being the source of truth.

That's where I'm strongest. I build HubSpot systems that are clean, scalable, documented, and easy for teams to use. I care about the architecture behind the work because that's what makes automation, reporting, segmentation, and handoffs actually function.

I'm a builder by default. I like clean systems, clear ownership, and processes that run without someone having to babysit them. If something has to be done manually more than once, it probably needs a better system.

Outside of work, I've got a golden retriever and a chow chow mix, who are my best friends.
