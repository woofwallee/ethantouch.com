About

Ethan Touch

You're looking for someone who can own HubSpot as a system, a source of truth for the teams relying on it every day. They need someone who can manage it at that level: permissions, architecture, cross-team support, integrations.

I'm Ethan. 4x HubSpot certified across marketing, sales, inbound, and reporting. Seven years in marketing ops, five of those owning HubSpot end to end, both in-house and on the agency side.

The problem that usually brings someone like me in is a platform that grew faster than its structure. Properties become a wasteland. Workflows start overlapping. Integrations break and nobody documents it. Teams start building their own workarounds, and eventually the system stops being something people trust.

I'm a builder by default. I like things working cleanly and automatically, and I'll put in the time to get there, because doing something manually twice is already once too many.

Outside of work, I've got a golden retriever and a chow chow mix, who are my best friends.
