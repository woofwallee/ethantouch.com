Field Notes / Email Design

Four redesigns. Four creative directors.

A short collection of speculative email redesigns built across 2025–26. None were paid engagements — each is a concept piece showing what the brand’s email program could look like under tighter art direction. One Memorial Weekend pet-care promo. One nonprofit policy newsletter. One association magazine. One B2B energy summit.

01
Pet Care · Memorial Weekend Boarding

The Ultimate Dog Daycare

A polished pet-care brand microsite for Collars Dog Resort’s Memorial Weekend boarding promo. Montserrat all-caps headlines, teal and golden mustard palette, circular framed photo with paw watermarks, handwritten Caveat tagline in the masthead, gold pill CTA, and a Pitch Best of KC 2026 vote-for-us module with four nomination links. Brand pulled directly from the live Collars site.

Brand
Collars Dog Resort, Olathe KS
Direction
Modern photographic pet-care brand
Type
Promotional · Single-CTA conversion
